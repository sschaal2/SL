=============================================================================

Author
Stefan Schaal
Date
Nov 2009
Remarks

Manages visualization of data traces on an oscilloscope, which can be a real physical oscilloscope or a graphics simulation of an oscilloscope. Additionally, for graphics oscilloscope, other data traces can be visualized.
