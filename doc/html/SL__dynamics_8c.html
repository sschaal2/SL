Note
compute_independent_joint_forces
Date
Sept 2010
Remarks

computes the generalized joint forces due to friction and spring terms, i.e., the sum of all forces that act per joint independently of all others. The sign of the terms is as if they were on the LEFT side of the RBD equations:

M qdd + C qd + G + f = u

Function Parameters: [in]=input,[out]=output

Parameters
[in]state: the joint state of the robot
[in]li: the link parameters for this joint

returns the generalized joint force for this joint due friction and spring terms
